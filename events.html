Málaga 451: The Night of the Books (Málaga, Andalucía)

On the route of Spanish free literary festivals, we arrive in Andalusia, specifically in Malaga. Writers, booksellers, publishers and readers have already been publishing four editions in Málaga 451: La noche de los libros , a literary festival in which there is also room for theater and music. In 2018 it was celebrated on May 11, and in addition to talking about the most current literature, they paid tribute to Jorge Luis Borges through a large exhibition in which his widow, María Kodama, collaborated. There is nothing. If you are a lover of the novel, poetry, comics or essay, you can enjoy that magical night for free! Do you think about losing it?

Festival of Fantasy of Fuenlabrada (Fuenlabrada, Madrid)

If you have a predilection for the fantastic genre , you will love the Fuenlabrada Fantasy Festival. And as every year they focus on a theme (epic fantasy, steampunk , the world of comics), they always want to return. In 2019 it will be celebrated on June 21, 22 and 23, and the theme chosen is the mythological Japan. Workshops, round tables, market troll, contest of illustration and stories and role-playing games are some of the many activities that are carried out in this literary festival free and free entry, which perfectly combines the didactic part with the playful.

Eñe Festival (Madrid)

The Eñe Festival is one of the most popular free literary festivals. In its last edition, held from October 15 to 25, it exceeded fourteen thousand attendees, so imagine. In 2018, Malaga joined as host, and hosted this great literary meeting on October 26 and 27.

Among the talks that took place last year, I highlight the one that focused on the proofreader as an irreplaceable piece in the editorial process , that of the national awards and the one that addressed the thorny issue of living in the story, that is, the money that really the writers win. And among the invited authors, I would have loved to see Samanta Schweblin , Rosa Montero, Elvira Lindo, Gioconda Belli and Almudena Grandes. And the entrance is free (until full capacity) to enjoy such a team! I have no choice but to aim the Eñe Festival on the list of free literary festivals that I have to attend at least once.

ENDEI: National Meeting of Independent Publishers (Castellón de la Plana, Comunidad Valenciana)

ENDEI was my first free literary festival. I attended its second edition, back in 2016, and I participated in its speed datings with editors to present my novel. Although I made a staging the sea of ​​pestle , I could not get any publisher decided to publish it, but I know of some authors and editors who have signed on that type of appointment. So if you cheer up, maybe you'll have more luck than me. Anyway, it helped me a lot to know how to talk about my novel and meet lovely people from the publishing world. In addition, I went to several round tables and talks, which gave rise to very intense debates.

The 2019 edition, which will be the fifth edition, will be held from March 14 to 16 in Castellón . Admission is free and free, but to participate in speed datings , whether as a writer, proofreader , translator or illustrator, you must sign up. If you are interested, run !, because the registration closes next March 4 .
